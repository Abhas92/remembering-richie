01

That Smile

One look and you knew — Richie was the warmest person in any room. That gentle, easy smile wasn't something he put on. It was simply who he was. The kind of person who made you feel like everything was going to be alright, just by being there. He didn't need to say a word — his presence alone was a comfort, a joy, a gift.

02

Mama's Boy, Always

From hilltop temples to seaside sunsets, Richie and his mother shared a bond that needed no words. He was the son who called, who showed up, who made her laugh on ordinary days and held her close on extraordinary ones. Whether they were exploring the mountains together or simply sitting side by side — the love between them was something everyone around could feel.

"To love and be loved — that is enough."
03

Heart of the Family

Family was everything to Abeer. He was the son who made his parents proud with quiet grace, the brother who always showed up when it mattered, and the cousin everyone wanted beside them at every celebration. Whether dressed in suits on a green lawn or huddled together for a selfie — where Richie was, family was whole.

04

His Little World

Then came the chapter that made his smile even brighter — becoming a father. The man who lit up every room now had a little one looking up at him with the same eyes. Walking hand-in-hand down a quiet street, matching outfits at family celebrations — Richie poured the same boundless love he gave everyone into the family he built. His little world was his greatest pride.

"Where there's family, there's home."
05

The Adventurer

Richie didn't just dream about adventures — he lived them. From helicopter rides over autumn forests to kayaking across city skylines, from fly-fishing in crystal rivers to standing at the edge of thundering waterfalls — he chased every experience with that unmistakable spark. At Pangong Lake, on mountain trails, under open skies — he made the whole world feel like home.

06

Celebrations & Joy

From vibrant Indian weddings draped in marigolds and colour, to intimate garden ceremonies under open skies — Richie was at the heart of every celebration. He didn't just attend; he brought the energy. In yellow kurtas and dark suits alike, surrounded by flowers and family, his laughter carried across every venue. Every gathering was warmer because he was part of it.

07

Together, Always

In white kurtas on a sunlit porch, babies in their arms, smiles wide and unguarded — this was Richie's world at its fullest. Brothers standing side by side, children playing at their feet, the whole family gathered not because they had to, but because they wanted to. These are the moments that will live forever — not in photographs, but in every heart that was part of them.

"Those we love don't go away,
they walk beside us every day.
Unseen, unheard, but always near —
still loved, still missed, and forever dear."
